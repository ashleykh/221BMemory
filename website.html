About Us

      "I consider that a man's brain originally is like a little empty attic, and you have to stock it with such furniture as you choose. . . ." - Sir Arthur Conan Doyle, the Adventures of Sherlock Holmes

      221b Memory is a K-12 detective-inspired interactive website that encourages memorization. Beginning January 1st, 2023, Sherlock Holmes was re-released as a public domain. In popular adaptations, the main motif is a mind palace--or rather, a memory palace. The method of loci, more commonly known as a memory palace, is a technique used to visualize associations to terms/definition to better comprehend concepts and stick to memory.

Visualize your studies with the mind palace!
